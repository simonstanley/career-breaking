Profile

Jack is a world recognised creative with a career that took off when he was just sixteen. His vast knowledge of various movement styles and his intuitive and innovative use of adapting movement for various mediums including TV, film, fashion, theatre and advertising has gained him huge respect within the industry.

He started off his training as a dancer in London, then soon found himself in Los Angeles where he was training and working with the best in the world. Since then he has choreographed and worked with Justin Bieber, JLS, Hozier, Katy Perry, Nike, Adidas, Honda, X-Factor, Comic-con, BMW and continues to collaborate and work with pioneering artists and brands such as these.

His knowledge of movement is ever evolving and spans a vast range of genres, including; Jazz, Contemporary, Street Dance, Hip-Hop (Popping, Locking, Breaking) and Acrobatics enabling Jack to always surpass the clients expectations by breaking moulds and continuing to set new trends that have a long lasting rippling effect.

Jack has travelled globally working on a vast spectrum of projects, making lifelong contacts along the way that are simply irreplaceable, enabling him to source from the most diverse and highly demanded creative artists.
